Special Services

Sri Sai Hospital provides exceptional special services, offering advanced medical care, compassionate treatment, and personalized attention, ensuring optimal health outcomes for all patients with specialized needs.

Emergency

Our emergency department provides rapid assessment and immediate care for injuries, illnesses, and emergencies, ensuring optimal outcomes.

General physician

Our physicians provide comprehensive primary care, focusing on prevention, accurate diagnosis, and effective treatment plans.

Orthopedic Surgeon

Our general surgeons perform abdominal, breast, and thyroid surgeries, ensuring faster recovery and improved patient outcomes.

 

General Surgeon

Our general surgeons perform abdominal, breast, and thyroid surgeries, prioritizing minimally invasive techniques for faster recovery.

Cardiologist

Our cardiologists manage hypertension, coronary artery disease, and heart failure, using advanced diagnostics to enhance health.

Dermatologist

Our dermatologists treat skin, hair, and nail conditions, providing personalized care for acne, eczema, skin cancer, and more.

Gastroenterologist

Our gastroenterologists treat digestive disorders like IBS, Crohn's disease, and liver conditions with advanced diagnostic and treatment options.

Vascular Surgeon

Our vascular surgeons treat arterial, venous, and lymphatic diseases, offering procedures for varicose veins, aneurysms, and PAD.

Ophthalmologist

Our ophthalmologists provide eye care, including exams, cataract, glaucoma, and retinal surgeries, enhancing and preserving vision.

Neuro/Psychiatrist

Our neuro/psychiatrists treat mental health and neurological disorders like depression, anxiety, epilepsy, and Parkinson's with holistic care.

Neurosurgeon

Our neurosurgeons perform surgeries for brain, spine, and peripheral nerve disorders, using cutting-edge techniques for optimal recovery.[

Urologist

Our urologists treat urinary and male reproductive conditions like kidney stones, infections, prostate issues, and male infertility.

Gynecologist

Our obstetricians and gynecologists provide care for women’s health, including prenatal care, labor, delivery, and gynecological surgeries.

Plastic Surgeon

Our plastic surgeons specialize in reconstructive and cosmetic procedures, offering corrective surgeries and aesthetic treatments for natural results.

Pediatric Surgeon

Our pediatric surgeons perform surgeries for infants, children, and adolescents, addressing congenital and acquired conditions with compassion.

Pulmonologist

Our pulmonologists treat asthma, COPD, and lung infections, offering advanced diagnostics and therapies to improve respiratory health.

Radiologist

Our radiologists use X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans to diagnose and monitor conditions, ensuring accurate imaging for effective treatment.

ENT

Our ENT specialists treat ear, nose, and throat conditions like sinusitis, hearing loss, and voice disorders, providing comprehensive care.

Anesthesia

Our anesthesia team ensures patient safety and comfort during surgery, tailoring anesthesia services to each patient's specific needs.

Facilities We Provide

Our state-of-the-art hospital boasts modern operation theaters, a well-equipped ICU, and 24/7 emergency care. With pharmacy and lab services, a dialysis center, and a dental clinic on-site, patients receive comprehensive care in hygienic wards, ensuring their health needs are met efficiently and effectively.
